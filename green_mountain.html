Green Mountain

Since discovering Arabica, humans have come to know Green Mountain as a holy site. Featuring some of the most fertile soil found on the planet, the mountain has been the sole source of certain naturally-occurring variants of coffee bean.

Wildlife

  1. Mountain Zing (Primary coffee variant found only on the mountain)
  2. Salvation (Found elsewhere on Arabica, this was the first coffee discovered by early settlers)
  3. Arabica Gold (Coffee Variant occuring near bodies of water)
  4. Animals resembling Earth's tortoises make the mountain their home, but are significantly faster than their earthly cousins
  5. Various twitchy animals; categorization has been difficult due to the impossibility of capturing specimens

Trivia

  • Many of the coffees growing on Green Mountain have demonstrated medicinal properties -- others have been shown to have hallucinogenic effects on persons with previously low levels of exposure to caffeine.
  • The first settlers to attempt drinking from the waters of Green Mountain were said to experience a manic burst of energy, causing them to run uncontrollably from the scene. They were never seen again.
